Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 04, 2024

Welcome to InstaZoom. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

Our extension does not collect personal data. We utilize local storage solely to manage your preferences for enabling or disabling the zoom feature.

Use of Your Information

The information stored in local storage is used exclusively to control the activation state of the zoom feature. No personal or sensitive information is shared with third parties.

Data Retention

Data is retained in local storage only as long as necessary to provide the zoom functionality. You can clear this data by uninstalling the extension or manually deleting your preferences from local storage.

Data Security

As no personal data is processed or stored beyond local storage, additional security measures beyond standard browser protections are not required.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy regularly.
